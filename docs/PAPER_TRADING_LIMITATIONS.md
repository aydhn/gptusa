# Paper Trading Limitations

The local paper trading foundation in the USA Signal Bot is strictly designed for **historical research, simulation, and workflow testing**.

**CRITICAL LIMITATIONS:**
1. **NO BROKER EXECUTION:** The system cannot, and will not, execute live or demo trades on any brokerage.
2. **Artificial Fills:** Fill prices are based strictly on local static cache files (OHLCV). There is no bid/ask spread insight, no order book depth, and no high-frequency market data.
3. **No Latency Impact:** The system does not simulate the time it takes an order to reach a market exchange.
4. **Static Slippage & Fees:** Slippage and fees are modeled as flat basis points. This does not map accurately to periods of extreme market volatility or illiquidity.
5. **No Market Impact:** Simulated large block orders do not move the simulated market.
6. **No Investment Advice:** The outcomes generated by the Paper Trading engine are purely hypothetical mathematical outputs. They are not guaranteed predictors of future success.
