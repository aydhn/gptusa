# Backtest Limitations and Disclaimers

The USA Signal Bot's backtesting engine is designed for local, theoretical research and comes with several stringent limitations.

## Strict Warnings
1. **No Guarantee of Future Performance**: Backtest results are strictly theoretical simulations and **do not** guarantee future trading profits.
2. **Not Financial Advice**: Output generated by the engine should not be construed as investment advice.
3. **No Live Execution**: The engine does not connect to broker APIs (like Alpaca, IBKR) and does not route real or paper orders to market venues.

## Algorithmic Limitations
* **Market Impact & Partial Fills**: Phase 25 assumes perfect liquidity where orders are fully filled without causing market impact. Real trading involves partial fills and orderbook dynamics.
* **Slippage & Fees**: While supported, these are modeled simplistically. Complex slippage depending on volatility or volume is not modeled in Phase 25.
* **Shorting & Margin**: Shorting, leverage, and margin trading are unsupported by default. The engine is long-only in this phase.
* **Survivorship Bias**: The engine utilizes local cached ticker data (e.g., via yfinance). If the universe definition omits delisted assets, your backtest will suffer from survivorship bias.
* **Lookahead Bias Risks**: Although `NEXT_OPEN` logic mitigates same-bar lookahead bias, certain multi-timeframe or look-forward indicators (if improperly constructed in earlier pipelines) can still cause bias.
* **Corporate Actions**: Dividends, splits, and delistings rely solely on the underlying cache provider's pre-adjustments and are not natively modeled as explicit events.
