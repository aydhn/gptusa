# Robustness Limitations

## Critical Disclaimers
1. **Historical Reliance**: Monte Carlo and bootstrap simulations rely entirely on past distributions. They cannot predict structural shifts or black swan events.
2. **Independence Assumption**: Bootstrapping trades assumes they are independent. In reality, trades cluster in regimes (e.g., trend following does well in prolonged trends).
3. **Execution Frictions**: Slippage and cost stress tests are hypothetical and may not capture actual market impact.
4. **Data Biases**: Survivorship bias and look-ahead bias vulnerabilities persist if the underlying historical data is flawed.
5. **No Live Approval**: A "VERY_ROBUST" score is a metric of historical stability, NOT an approval to run live trades.

**THIS IS A RESEARCH SYSTEM ONLY. ROBUSTNESS METRICS ARE NOT INVESTMENT ADVICE OR GUARANTEES.**
